Nisr Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 13, 2025

Welcome to Nisr! This Privacy Policy explains how Nisr Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application and services (collectively, the "Service").

Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in a few different ways to provide and improve our Service.

A. Information You Provide to Us

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect information depending on your sign-up method.

  • Email Sign-up: We collect your full name, email address, and password.
  • Phone Sign-up: We collect your full name and phone number.

Optional Profile Information: You can choose to add more information to your profile, such as your location, gender, a profile picture, a shop name, shop picture, and shop location.

Content You Post: We collect the information you provide in the ads you post, including descriptions, photos, and prices.

Communications: When you contact us for support or other inquiries, we collect the information you provide in your messages.

B. Information from Third-Party Services

If you create an account using Google or Facebook, we receive information from those services.

  • Google: We receive your name, email address, profile picture, and Google ID.
  • Facebook: We receive your name, profile picture, and Facebook ID.

C. Information We Collect Automatically

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our Service, such as the features you use, the pages you view, and the time and date of your visits.

Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access our Service, including your IP address, operating system, device model, and your Google Advertising ID (AAID).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Manage the Service: To create and maintain your account, publish your advertisements, and enable you to communicate with other users.
  • To Improve and Personalize the Service: To understand how our users interact with Nisr, develop new features, and customize the content and ads you see.
  • To Communicate With You: To send you service-related notifications, respond to your support requests, and send you marketing communications that you can opt-out of.
  • For Safety and Security: To prevent fraud, enforce our Terms of Use, and protect the security of our platform and users.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: To meet any applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your personal information.

  • Access and Update: You can access and update most of your account information directly in the app's settings.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time through the app settings or by contacting us. When you delete your account, we will remove your public-facing data and take steps to delete your account information, subject to our data retention policies and legal obligations.
  • Communications: You can opt out of receiving marketing emails by following the unsubscribe link in the email. You can also manage push notifications in your device settings.

Your information is stored on secure servers provided by Google Cloud. This means your data may be stored in locations outside of Ethiopia. We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your personal data from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access or disclosure.

Our Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.
